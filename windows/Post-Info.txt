Activation Manual (VIPHACKER100 Edition):
	1. Run the Loader (Run-Burp.bat)
	2. Click "Run" in the Loader window to launch Burp Suite
	3. Copy the License string from the Loader to Burp Suite and click Next
	4. Click "Manual Activation" in Burp Suite
	5. Copy Activation Request from Burp Suite to the Loader
	6. Copy the generated Activation Response from the Loader back to Burp Suite
	7. Click Next — you're done! ;)
